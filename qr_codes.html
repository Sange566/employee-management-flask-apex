How to Use QR Code Login
For Mobile Users:
  1. Generate the appropriate QR code
  2. Save the QR code image to your phone
  3. Go to the login page on your computer
  4. Click "Scan QR Code" and use your phone's camera
  5. Point your phone's camera at the QR code
  6. You'll be automatically logged in!
For Desktop Users:
  1. Generate the appropriate QR code
  2. Print the QR code or display it on another device
  3. Go to the login page
  4. Click "Scan QR Code"
  5. Allow camera access when prompted
  6. Point your camera at the QR code
  7. You'll be automatically logged in!
Pro Tip: QR codes work immediately and don't expire. Save them for future use!
